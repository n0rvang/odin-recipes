Lasagna

Homemade lasagna to die for

Lasagna is an Italian dish which is very rich in flavor, lasagna is one of the oldest pasta dishes there are. Made by meat, cheese and spices, this dish will wow you away.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Here is a brief overview of what you can expect when you make homemade lasagna:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
